What is this?

Upvado, short for Upvado Software Solutions, is a small company I started to build eCommerce tools for platforms like Magento (Adobe Commerce), Shopify, and Shopware—and maybe more down the line. Who am I? I’m a certified Adobe Commerce Developer with over 10 years of experience building online stores using Magento. After working on all kinds of projects, I decided to start my own business. For now, it’s just me, but I’m excited to see where this goes.


My first project is a Magento 2 extension called "Upvado_PageRestriction." It solves a couple of common issues, like restricting pages based on customer groups and blocking certain pages for non-logged-in users. The plan is to release it on the Adobe Commerce Marketplace by the end of 2024.


Next year, I’m planning to work on integrations for platforms like Etsy, Shopee, and Allegro. These tools will be especially helpful for smaller stores that don’t have fancy systems for managing their product data and use Magento as their main source of truth. My goal is to make life easier for businesses that want to sell on multiple platforms without all the hassle.


This website isn’t fully ready yet because I’m focusing on building and releasing extensions. Once things are further along, I’ll update this page. Thanks for stopping by!
